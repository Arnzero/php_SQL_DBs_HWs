Three rules-of-thumb that a web designer should know
- Keep each page's text short
- Put the most important content at the top of the page
- Choose fonts and font sizes carefully

Three more rules-of-thumb that a web designer should know
- Write alternative text for your images and non-text content
- Provide search if the site has more than 100 pages
- Users do not read, they are skimmers, google is precious
